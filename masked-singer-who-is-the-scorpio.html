The Masked Singer season 9 has been full of surprises and MASSIVE talent and stars! And this week, we’re being introduced to the first astrology-based costume we’ve ever seen on the show. The Scorpio looks like they’re bringing big DIVA energy with that bright gold from head to toe.

So which celebrity could the Scorpio be under the mask? And what are the clues pointing to their identity? Here’s what we know so far about the Scorpio.

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 9

Scorpio’s appearance on the show started with her walking on and all the judges commenting on how small she is! In her clues, she’s standing in front of a red car and mentions that she loves “being over the top.” We see her dancing in front of a “CLAW STREET” sign. Growing up in a small town, she knew she was built for bigger things. The camera pans in to a collection of sports balls while one of the men in black talks on a phone. She hustled, working in fast food and bars to buy her way “out of the sticks and closer to [her] destiny.” We see a crumpled dollar bill fall to the floor.

Finally, she made her way West and got to “work, clawing [her] way into the industry.” But after years of being other people, the key was just to be herself, even if that makes her the villain sometimes! A computer screen displayed “SCORP10” as a stock symbol. Next, we see a framed photo of a house. The gossip says she’s a “wild thing,” she claims while men in black hold up measuring tape and a manilla folder.

After her performance, some dancers did the sprinkler as a clue! Scorpio explained, “I’m never the one doing it, but even I know that watering your property is important.”

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Scorpio

Robin predicted this could be a reality star or housewife. Jenny guessed Erika Jayne, Lisa Rinna, or Denise Richards. Ken thought it might be Heidi Klum because of the fashion clues. Nicole noticed her spice and the fact that she could do the splits, so while she didn’t have a guess right away, she didn’t agree with the Heidi Klum guess.

Online, people thought this might be someone from Selling Sunset or Million Dollar Listing. Another popular guess was also Heather Morris from Glee!

Who is the Scorpio on The Masked Singer?

With the some stiff competition from the Moose and the Doll, Scorpio was eliminated on 80’s Night. She was revealed to be…

Christine Quinn from Selling Sunset!

So fun to see her take on The Masked Singer stage! And she even capped off her performance with the splits. What a ride!
