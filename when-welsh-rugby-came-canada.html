When Welsh Rugby came to Canada

The Women's Rugby World Cup has just kicked off in New Zealand and we're incredibly excited to support and follow the Welsh team! To warm up for the tournament the Wales Women's Team visited Halifax, Nova Scotia in August to take part in a week's training camp before taking to the Wanderers Ground to face the Canadian national team.

While Wales were defeated 31-3 to a team ranked fourth in the world, that did not dampen the spirits of the Welsh supporters at the ground and we had a great time cheering on the team with fellow Welsh and Canadian friends living in Halifax.
