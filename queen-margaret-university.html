Queen Margaret University, Edinburgh | Scotland.org

QMU offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses, taught in a friendly campus environment with easy access to Scotland’s capital city. The University has a rich history dating back to 1875, and continues to be true to its original aim of responding to some of the most pressing needs of society and improving quality of life. Today, QMU offers the optimum balance of theoretical and practical education, allowing you to become your best self and go far..
With accommodation, academic, sports and students’ union buildings on one manageable campus, QMU offers a comfortable and convenient study and living environment for approximately 5000 students where you need never be lost in the crowd.
