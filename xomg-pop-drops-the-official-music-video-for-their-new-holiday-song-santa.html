XOMG POP! Drops The Official Music Video For Their New Holiday Song 'SANTA'

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by Tommaso Boddi/Getty Images for Skechers

Reading Time: 2 minutes

JoJo Siwa’s pop group XOMG POP! has released a festive new music video for their holiday track “SANTA” that shows the four members getting into the Christmas spirit. The video features group members Penelope, Dallas, Brooklyn, and Tinie T baking cookies, wrapping presents, and celebrating the holidays together.

XOMG POP! Released a Holiday Themed Music Video

Throughout the video, only Penelope seems to see Santa Claus himself, constantly getting excited and running over whenever he appears. The other girls look confused as Penelope receives magical gifts from thin air and keeps waving at someone they can’t see.

Beyond the playful “SANTA” premise, the video shows the singers partaking in classic holiday activities such as baking cookies and wrapping presents. The song itself exudes holiday cheer, with jingle bells providing a festive backdrop for the girls’ bubbly voices.

Of course, the group has JoJo Siwa to thank for assembling this dazzling array of young talent. She serves as their mentor and inspiration for pursuing their pop star dreams. When asked about JoJo in a recent interview, new member Penelope called it “crazy” that she gets to work with the YouTube icon on a regular basis.

XOMG POP! was formed through Siwa’s reality competition show Siwas Dance Pop Revolution in 2021. The multitalented mogul hand-picked the original members to form the next big girl group, and fans have embraced their catchy songs and flashy music videos.

The holiday release marks the group’s first new music endeavor with Penelope, who seems to be fitting right if the “SANTA” video is any indication. The 10-year-old certainly brings her strong dance abilities and bubbly energy to the already bright and charismatic group.

XOMG POP! Will Be Going on Tour

After landing a hit holiday single with “SANTA,” XOMG POP! has an exciting 2024 lined up. They recently announced a summer headlining tour kicking off in June.

The ten-city trek will take the energetic foursome to major venues across the country. Stops include iconic theaters in Minneapolis, Boston, Atlanta, Orlando, and more. The tour will be wrapping up in Dallas, Texas in late June.

Fans can expect to hear the girls’ bouncy catalog of originals, like the fan-favorite tracks “Merry Go Round” and “Candy Hearts.” They will also likely showcase some cover songs and new yet-to-be-released music.
