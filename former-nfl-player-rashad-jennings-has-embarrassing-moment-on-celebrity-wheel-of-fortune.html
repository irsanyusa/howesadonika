Former NFL Player Rashad Jennings Has Embarrassing Moment on 'Celebrity Wheel of Fortune'

Former NFL running back and Dancing With The Stars Season 24 winner, Rashad Jennings, recently found himself in a rather embarrassing situation. The mishap occurred during an episode of Celebrity Wheel of Fortune when he attempted to solve a puzzle involving the iconic movie director Quentin Tarantino.

Rashad Jennings Failed To Guess One Letter on Celebrity Wheel of Fortune

Rashad Jennings recently took the Celebrity Wheel of Fortune stage alongside fellow NFL stars Marcellus Wiley and Jared Allen. During the episode, one of the puzzles required Jennings to complete the phrase “DRIVING TO RENO WITH QUENTIN TARANTINO.” This saying was under the “rhyme” category. By the time Jennings was going to guess, he only needed the letter “Q” to spell out Tarantino’s first name. However, instead of choosing the obvious “Q,” he confidently blurted out the letter “P.”

Host Pat Sajak‘s response was a straightforward, “No, No, I’m sorry no.” A moment of laughter from someone in the background underscored the blunder. Jennings had $4,050 in winnings at that point, and his error left him without the chance to solve the puzzle.

Jennings’ mistake didn’t go unnoticed by the show’s viewers. Social media users wasted no time, they immediately fled to X/Twitter to poke fun at him. Some called it the “worst Wheel of Fortune blunder ever,” while others questioned if he even knew the rules of the game. A few kind souls offered a bit of understanding, suggesting that not knowing Tarantino’s name might have played a role in the mishap.

Despite the humorous incident, Jennings didn’t take it to heart and even playfully teased his appearance on the show before it aired. He also spoke about his foundation, The Rashad Jennings Foundation, which focuses on health, leadership, mentorship, and reading.

The Incident Led to “Puentin Tarantino” Jokes Going Viral

Jennings’ unforgettable blunder on Wheel of Fortune led to an explosion of “Puentin Tarantino” jokes across social media. The gaffe was so glaring, it left both the audience and Sajak in disbelief.

Social media users wasted no time in having a field day with the mishap. “Puentin Tarantino” quickly became a trending topic, with people creating memes and playful jabs at Jennings’ unexpected guess. While some were rather harsh, the overall tone seemed to be more lighthearted, highlighting the humorous side of the situation.

Jennings, a former NFL player with a respectable career and an impressive victory on DWTS Season 24, took the situation in stride. His good-natured response and willingness to join in on the fun allowed people to see the lighter side of the incident.
