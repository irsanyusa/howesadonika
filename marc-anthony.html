Marc Anthony (Salsa and Pop Singer-Songwriter)

Full Name: Marco Antonio Muñiz
Profession: Salsa and Pop Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Marc Anthony is the top-selling salsa artist of all time, selling over 12 million albums, including "Contra La Corriente" (1997), "3.0" (2013) and "Opus" (2019).

Born and raised in New York, Anthony has released albums in both Spanish and English and won numerous music awards - his first Grammy win was in 1998 for "Contra La Corriente" for Best Latin Tropical Performance. In 2013 he received the Latin Grammy Award for Record of the Year for "Vivir Mi Vida".

The multi-talented performer is also an actor, producer, dancer and fashion designer. In 1998 she appeared on stage in Paul Simon and Derek Walcott's musical "The Capeman". In 2007 he portrayed salsa legend Héctor Lavoe in "El Cantante", alongside his then wife, Jennifer Lopez.
