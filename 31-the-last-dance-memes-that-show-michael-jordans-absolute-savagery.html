31 The Last Dance Memes That Show Michael Jordan's Absolute Savagery

In the latest episodes, people have begun to really notice a pattern: MJ creating motivation for himself at any cost. Did you say something to him? He's coming for you. Did you not say anything to him? He's coming for you. The man is a walking Catch-22 of basketball vengeance, which is just spectacular.

Below, take a look at some of the funniest memes to come out of the last few weeks of ESPN's The Last Dance. Hopefully you laugh as much at these as Michael Jordan did at the thought of Gary Payton getting the best of him.

Many have begun noticing the pattern of MJ's self motivation. Seems like there's no real way around, to be honest.
