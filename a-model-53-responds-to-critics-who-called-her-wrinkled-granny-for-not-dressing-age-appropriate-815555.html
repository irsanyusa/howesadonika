A Model, 53, Responds to Critics Who Called Her Wrinkled Granny For Not Dressing Age Approp

In a world that often fixates on youth and superficial standards of beauty, one resilient and confident 53-year-old model has decided to redefine the narrative. Meet the inspiring individual who has gracefully shrugged off the critics who labeled her a “wrinkled granny” for her refusal to conform to conventional notions of “age-appropriate” dressing.

Luisa Dunn embarked on her gray hair journey with a compelling challenge.

Luisa Dunn is a woman on a mission, driven by a deep desire to challenge prevailing norms of ageism and conventional beauty standards. As a model, she’s determined to infuse the fashion and modeling industry with a breath of fresh air. Her remarkable journey into embracing her natural gray hair began with a simple realization: the repetitive process of coloring her hair every 14 days no longer brought her joy.

In her candid interview, she expressed, “Toward the end, it felt like I was on a cover-up merry-go-round that only exacerbated the negative feelings I was having about aging, self-acceptance, and my changing body.” With unwavering resolve, she made the life-changing decision to bid farewell to hair dye, a choice she’s never looked back on with regret.

The model has grown weary of age-based clichés and boldly voices her opposition to them.

Dunn became increasingly exasperated by a specific stereotype that equates “gray with old,” recognizing the potential long-term effects this assumption could have on anyone’s sense of self. Fortunately, she discovered a supportive community of like-minded women on Instagram, who became an additional wellspring of inspiration along her path.

For Dunn, embracing her gray hair has since become synonymous with practicing self-love. She articulated her journey: “Learning to love myself and my changing body unapologetically was the answer to strengthening my confidence and self-esteem.”

Transitioning to her natural gray hair was yet another significant challenge for her.

Surprisingly, the practical aspect of allowing her silver hair to flourish wasn’t as straightforward for Dunn as she had initially imagined. She candidly shared, “I discovered my first gray hair at 16 and, as a result, I started coloring my roots to hide them using dark and light brown tones. It wasn’t until 2019 when I decided I was done with trying to hide them, and I wanted to opt for lighter, cooler tones to match my roots.”

She further explained, “The process is undeniably lengthy and demands considerable patience, especially if you embrace the transition without any shortcuts. Typically, it takes most women two or more years to complete this transformation. A few months into my journey, I found myself compelled to bid farewell to the bleach-damaged lengths, embracing a pixie grow-out as part of the process.”

Luisa Dunn displayed remarkable grace and confidence when faced with mean comments about her age.

In response to a rude comment on one of her TikTok videos, where she was labeled a “wrinkled granny,” she chose a path of maturity and self-assuredness. Rather than resorting to insults or anger, this influencer responded with a touch of humor and humility.

Luisa acknowledged the presence of wrinkles but maintained her youthful spirit, expressing her excitement at becoming a granny someday, should fortune allow it. Her response not only defused the negativity but also showcased her resilience and ability to rise above hurtful remarks with a dignified and positive attitude.

Luisa Dunn imparts a potent piece of wisdom to her admirers.

Dunn sincerely believes that the current decade stands as the pinnacle of fashion. She asserts, “I feel that individual style is celebrated more now, and clothing is seen as just another layer of our personalities. We can dress for our moods or simply to have fun. There is less focus on rules and more emphasis on being yourself and having fun with fashion.”

Among the myriad beauty insights she imparts to her followers, there’s one particularly precious nugget of wisdom: the importance of maintaining a playful spirit, especially when it comes to makeup. She elaborated, saying, “It’s important to have fun every now and again, so try things that are outside the box. What’s the worst that can happen? You’ll just have to remove it. At times, makeup should be for pure joy and playfulness — anything goes in my book, providing you feel good.”

In a world where societal beauty standards often overlook the wisdom and beauty that comes with age, another model is taking a stand apart from Dunn. Learn how Paulina Porizkova responded to critics who labeled her a “desperate grandma” after sharing empowering bikini photos. Her story is a testament to confidence, resilience, and the timeless pursuit of self-love.
