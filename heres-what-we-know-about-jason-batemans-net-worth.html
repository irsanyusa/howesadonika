Here's What We Know About Jason Bateman's Net Worth

Jason Bateman 's deadpan delivery and clever sense of humor has made him a household name, but the actor is definitely not one for resting on his laurels. Bateman is also a director and producer, and the 2019 Primetime Emmy Awards saw him snag an award for Outstanding Directing for a Drama Series for Ozark . Earlier that year, Bateman had also won a Screen Actors Guild statuette for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Drama Series.

Jason Bateman's net worth is $30 million .

Bateman started his career on TV in the early '80s when he starred on Little House on the Prairie, and he later became a teen idol in the mid-80s when he starred as David Hogan on The Hogan Family. He then became the Directors Guild of America 's youngest-ever director when he directed three episodes of The Hogan Family when he was just 18.

And while Bateman had a brief career slump in the '90s, 2003 saw him star in the fan-favorite comedy series Arrested Development, and he would go on to win a SAG Award, a Golden Globe, and an Emmy for his work on the series. He's also the richest of the Arrested Development cast . Ozark later premiered in 2017, and 2020's The Outsider was an instant ratings hit .
